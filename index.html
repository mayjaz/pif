Who We Are

We are the official distributor of Ingrain products in Zimbabwe. Ingrain is Africa’s largest producer of starch and other related products. Utilising maize and cassava, Ingrain manufactures a wide range of products for an equally diverse variety of industries such as food, beverages, pharmaceuticals, textiles, paper making and adhesives. We make these products available here in Zimbabwe for these industries and applications.
